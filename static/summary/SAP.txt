SAP SE (/ɛseɪˈpi/) (Systeme, Anwendungen und Produkte in der Datenverarbeitung; "Systems, Applications & Products in Data Processing") is a German multinational software corporation that makes enterprise software to manage business operations and customer relations.[2] SAP is headquartered in Walldorf, Baden-Württemberg, with regional offices in 180[3] countries.[4] The company has over 335,000 customers in over 180 countries.[4] The company is a component of the Euro Stoxx 50 stock market index.[5]
